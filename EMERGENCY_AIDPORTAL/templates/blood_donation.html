Your contribution can save a life!

Do not donate blood if you are under any medication or have a medical history.

Who Can Donate Blood?

  • - Age: 18-65 years
  • - Weight: At least 50 kg
  • - Healthy with no chronic diseases
  • - No recent surgeries or infections
  • - Not donated blood in the last 3 months
  • - No active medications that may interfere
  • - Hydrate well before donation
  • - Have a balanced meal before donating
